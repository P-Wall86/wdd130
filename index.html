My name is Pamela and I'm from Misiones, Argentina. Misiones is the land of one of the Seven Natural Wonders, shared with Brazil: The Iguazu Falls. I love music and I'm going back to practice hobbies I left behind like drawing and scale modeling.

Misiones

Misiones is one of the 23 provinces located in the northeast of Argentina. It borders Paraguay to the west and Brazil to the north and east. It is famous for the Iguazu Falls and green landscapes mixed with deep red-colour dirt.
